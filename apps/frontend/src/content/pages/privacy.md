---
title: Privacy
description: How we handle your personal health information
---

Your data belongs to you.

This application is built around a simple principle: information about your body is personal, and it should remain under your control.

## Data minimization

We intentionally collect as little personal information as possible.

The application does not ask for, and does not store:

- Your name
- Your date of birth
- Your age
- Your location
- Your gender
- Any demographic information
- Any profile information that describes who you are

There is no personal profile in the traditional sense.

Your account is identified only by the minimum technical information required for access (for example, an email address or login identifier).

## What data is stored

The application stores only:

- The information you choose to enter (such as daily observations and cycle-related entries)
- The minimum technical data required to keep your account and subscription working

No additional personal or demographic data is collected.

## How your data is used

Your data is used only to:

- Show you your own records
- Calculate and display results inside the app
- Provide the features you choose to use

Your data is not:

- Sold
- Rented
- Shared with advertisers
- Used for advertising
- Used for tracking across other websites or apps
- Used to build marketing or behavioral profiles

## No identity profiling

Because we do not collect demographic or identifying profile information, your data is not used to build any kind of personal profile about you.

The data you enter exists only to serve the function of the application for you.

## Access and control

You remain in control of your data at all times.

- You can delete your data from within the app
- You can stop using the service at any time
- When data is deleted, it is removed from active systems

## Third-party services

Some infrastructure services (such as hosting, email delivery, or payments) may process limited technical data as required to operate the service.

These providers are used only to run the application and are not allowed to use your data for their own purposes.

No third party is given access to your personal health data for advertising, marketing, or profiling.

## Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are taken to protect your data. No system can guarantee absolute security, but this information is treated with appropriate care.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published in the app or on the website.

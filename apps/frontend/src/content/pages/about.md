title: About
description: The philosophy and purpose behind this project
---

This project exists to provide a simple, structured way to record and review patterns in a cycle.

Many people find it difficult to apply general information in daily life. Bodies change over time, and factors such as stress, illness, travel, and changes in routine can affect patterns in ways that are not always predictable.

This application is designed as a focused tool for daily observation and long-term review. It allows users to record what they notice and look back over time to better understand how things change.

The goal is not to provide predictions, guarantees, or medical conclusions. The goal is to support awareness and careful observation.

## The product is intentionally built to be:

- Simple
- Conservative in its conclusions
- Clear about what it shows and what it does not claim

This application does not provide medical advice and is not a substitute for professional healthcare. Any decisions made using the information remain the responsibility of the user.

Some people may use a tool like this for a short time, others for longer, and some may return to it during different phases of life. That is expected.

Because this type of information is personal, privacy and data control are treated as core principles of the product.

This project is developed with a long-term perspective, with an emphasis on reliability, restraint, and respect for the complexity of the human body.

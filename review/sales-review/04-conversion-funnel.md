# Sales & Conversion Review: Conversion Funnel

## Title: Open Source as a Lead Magnet

**Severity:** Informational
**Confidence:** High
**Category:** Conversion Funnel
**Evidence:** 
The primary distribution currently is the GitHub repository.

**Current Experience:**
Users discover the repo, read the `README.md`, and either pull the Docker image or look for a hosted version.

**Business Impact:**
The open-source repository acts as a massive top-of-funnel lead magnet. Developers will star the repo, discuss it on Reddit, and rank it on Hacker News. Many of those developers will not want to maintain a Postgres instance and will gladly pay for the hosted SaaS version.

**Suggested Direction:**
Ensure the link to the hosted SaaS version is prominent at the very top of the `README.md`, offering a frictionless upgrade path from "Interested Developer" to "Paying Customer."

**Estimated Revenue Impact:** High
**Estimated Effort:** Low
**Launch Blocker:** No

# Sales & Conversion Review: Retention & Expansion

## Title: Building the Daily Habit

**Severity:** Informational
**Confidence:** Confirmed
**Category:** Retention & Expansion
**Evidence:** 
The app relies on the user logging data daily (BBT, mucus) to maintain accuracy.

**Current Experience:**
The core retention loop is biological. If the user wants to know their fertility status, they must log data. The app doesn't need fake "streaks" because the real-world value is the retention mechanism.

**Business Impact:**
Health utilities have naturally high retention if they are reliable. By keeping the logging process frictionless (taking only seconds), the app ensures high DAU (Daily Active Users).

**Suggested Direction:**
Positive finding. Do not artificially inflate retention with push notification spam.

**Estimated Revenue Impact:** High
**Estimated Effort:** N/A
**Launch Blocker:** No

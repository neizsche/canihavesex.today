# Sales & Conversion Review: Calls to Action (CTA)

## Title: Non-Manipulative Prompts

**Severity:** Informational
**Confidence:** High
**Category:** Calls to Action
**Evidence:** 
The application does not use dark patterns or countdown timers to force upgrades.

**Current Experience:**
If a user tries to edit a past entry while in demo mode, they see a clean, informative banner explaining the limitation rather than an aggressive full-screen modal demanding a credit card.

**Business Impact:**
Aggressive CTAs in a health app destroy trust. By keeping the upgrade paths polite and contextual, the application preserves its clinical, respectful tone.

**Suggested Direction:**
Positive finding. Maintain this respectful boundary.

**Estimated Revenue Impact:** Medium (increases LTV via trust)
**Estimated Effort:** N/A
**Launch Blocker:** No

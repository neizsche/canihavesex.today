# Sales & Conversion Review: Value Proposition

## Title: Privacy as a Premium Feature

**Severity:** Informational
**Confidence:** High
**Category:** Value Proposition
**Evidence:** 
The application's core architecture (self-hostable, zero telemetry) and direct messaging ("Answers one honest question").

**Current Experience:**
Users are bombarded with "free" fertility apps that monetize by selling data to data brokers. This product charges money directly in exchange for absolute privacy and a clean, non-gamified interface.

**Business Impact:**
The value proposition is extremely strong for a specific, growing niche (privacy-conscious individuals, post-Roe US residents, tech workers). It commands a premium price point because the alternative is fundamentally compromised.

**Suggested Direction:**
Positive finding. Double down on "You are the customer, not the product" in all sales copy.

**Estimated Effort:** N/A
**Launch Blocker:** No

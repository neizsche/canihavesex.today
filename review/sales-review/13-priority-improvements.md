# Sales & Conversion Review: Priority Improvements

This document ranks the conversion improvements based on their impact on sustainable revenue.

## Must Fix Before Release
1. **Marketing Landing Page:** As noted in the Brand Audit, the lack of a clear `index.html` marketing page means non-technical traffic hitting the root domain will bounce. A pricing table must be visible before the user attempts to sign up.

## Should Fix Soon (v1.x)
1. **Pricing Table Clarity:** The pricing table must explicitly list "Open Source" (Free, DIY) vs "Cloud Hosted" (Paid, Managed). This removes the objection "Why should I pay for free software?"
2. **14-Day Full Trial:** Before falling back to the restrictive "Demo" mode, give users 14 days of full historical access to prove the value of the charting features.
3. **"Cancel Anytime" Guarantee:** Ensure Stripe Customer Portal is linked prominently in the app settings so users know they aren't trapped in a subscription.

## Can Wait Until After v1.0 (v2.0+)
1. **Export Tool:** Build a dedicated data export feature (JSON/CSV) that is highlighted on the pricing page as a trust signal: "We don't hold your data hostage."
2. **Couples Sharing:** (Expansion revenue) Allow a user to invite a partner to view (read-only) their status for a small fee or as part of a higher-tier plan.

# Sales & Conversion Review: Revenue Risks

## Title: Over-Complicating Self-Hosting

**Severity:** Medium
**Confidence:** Confirmed
**Category:** Revenue Risks
**Evidence:** 
The repository offers a Docker setup.

**Current Experience:**
If self-hosting is too easy, no one pays for the cloud version. If self-hosting is too hard, people complain on Hacker News and the launch tanks.

**Business Impact:**
The current balance (Docker compose requiring some technical knowledge) is actually perfect. Non-technical users will fail to deploy it and will happily pay the $40/year for the cloud version, while engineers will deploy it for free and become brand advocates. 

**Suggested Direction:**
Do not build "1-click deploy to Vercel/Render" buttons. Leave the self-hosted path slightly technical (Docker) to naturally filter users who should be paying for the SaaS.

**Estimated Revenue Impact:** High
**Estimated Effort:** N/A
**Launch Blocker:** No

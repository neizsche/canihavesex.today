# Sales & Conversion Review: Objection Analysis

## Title: Handling "Why Should I Pay?"

**Severity:** Low
**Confidence:** Medium
**Category:** Objection Handling
**Evidence:** 
Self-hosters frequently ask why they should pay for an open-source product.

**Current Experience:**
The README states they can self-host for free. 

**Business Impact:**
If the marketing page doesn't explicitly justify the cost of the hosted version (server maintenance, continuous updates, secure backups, supporting the solo developer), many users will attempt to self-host, fail, and churn entirely instead of paying.

**Suggested Direction:**
Add an FAQ section on the pricing page: "Why should I pay for the cloud version?" Answer honestly: "Hosting Postgres and maintaining backups is hard. Paying for the cloud version supports development and ensures your data is securely backed up without you having to manage servers."

**Estimated Revenue Impact:** Medium
**Estimated Effort:** Low
**Launch Blocker:** No

# Clinical Review: Ethical Communication

## Title: Absolute Respect for User Autonomy

**Severity:** Informational
**Confidence:** Confirmed
**Category:** Ethical Communication
**Affected Files:** `TodayScreen.tsx`
**Evidence:** 
The app provides the raw data (cycle day, symptoms) alongside its algorithmic interpretation. It never restricts the user's ability to edit past data or forces them to accept the algorithm's conclusion.

**Scientific Assessment:**
In healthcare, the patient's lived experience must not be overridden by a black-box algorithm. The app functions as a tool that works *for* the user, not a medical authority issuing commands.

**Potential User Impact:**
Users retain ownership of their bodily observations.

**Suggested Direction:**
Positive finding. 

**Relevant Guideline or Evidence:** 
Medical Ethics Principle of Autonomy.
**Release Blocker:** No

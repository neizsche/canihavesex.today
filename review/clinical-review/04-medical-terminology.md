# Clinical Review: Medical Terminology

## Title: Accurate Phase and Symptom Terminology

**Severity:** Informational
**Confidence:** Confirmed
**Category:** Medical Terminology
**Affected Files:** `apps/backend/src/engine.ts`, `apps/frontend/src/components/log/LogPrimarySignals.tsx`
**Evidence:** 
The app utilizes standard clinical terminology for the menstrual cycle: "Follicular", "Ovulatory", "Luteal", and "Period". Cervical fluid observations are categorized appropriately: "dry", "sticky", "creamy", "watery", and "eggwhite".

**Scientific Assessment:**
These terms match standard symptothermal tracking charts and anatomical definitions. 

**Potential User Impact:**
Users familiar with FAM (Fertility Awareness Methods) will immediately understand the terminology without needing to translate colloquialisms.

**Suggested Direction:**
Positive finding. 

**Relevant Guideline or Evidence:** 
ACOG definitions of the menstrual cycle.
**Release Blocker:** No

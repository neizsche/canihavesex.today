# Clinical Review: Product Scope

## Title: Clear Distinction from Contraception

**Severity:** Informational
**Confidence:** Confirmed
**Category:** Scope of the Product
**Affected Files:** `README.md`, `TodayScreen.tsx`
**Evidence:** 
The README prominently displays: "Not medical advice, and not a contraceptive." The `TodayScreen` utilizes the terms "Not Fertile" rather than "Safe", and "Not Sure" rather than "Maybe".

**Scientific Assessment:**
Positioning the app strictly as an educational / awareness tool rather than a digital contraceptive removes significant regulatory burden (e.g., FDA classification as Software as a Medical Device - SaMD) and protects user safety.

**Potential User Impact:**
Users are less likely to mistakenly rely on the app as a foolproof method of pregnancy prevention.

**Suggested Direction:**
Positive finding. Maintain this strict separation in all future marketing copy.

**Relevant Guideline or Evidence:** FDA Guidance on Device Software Functions and Mobile Medical Applications.
**Release Blocker:** No

# Clinical Review: Clinical Safety

## Title: No Guarantees of Efficacy

**Severity:** Informational
**Confidence:** Confirmed
**Category:** Clinical Safety
**Affected Files:** `README.md`
**Evidence:** 
The application explicitly avoids quoting "99% effectiveness" rates.

**Scientific Assessment:**
Many commercial apps quote perfect-use Pearl Index rates (e.g., 99.4%) to sell subscriptions, ignoring that typical-use failure rates for fertility awareness are closer to 15-24% (CDC). By refusing to quote efficacy numbers, the application avoids misleading vulnerable users.

**Potential User Impact:**
Users cannot hold the application liable for unintended pregnancy based on marketed efficacy rates.

**Suggested Direction:**
Positive finding. Continue to refuse to quote contraceptive efficacy rates.

**Relevant Guideline or Evidence:** 
CDC Contraceptive Failure Rates.
**Release Blocker:** No

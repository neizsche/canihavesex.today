# Brand Review: Tone of Voice

## Title: Mature and Unapologetic

**Severity:** Informational
**Confidence:** Confirmed
**Category:** Tone of Voice
**Evidence:** 
The name "canihavesex.today" paired with clinical output like "Highly Fertile" and "Not Fertile".

**Current Experience:**
The brand does not beat around the bush. It acknowledges that the primary reason people track their fertility is to answer a binary question regarding sexual activity.

**Why It Matters:**
This brutal honesty is refreshing. It respects the user's intelligence and time. It doesn't pretend the app is a generalized "wellness companion."

**Suggested Direction:**
Positive finding.

# Brand Review: Emotional Journey

## Title: Relief Through Ownership

**Severity:** Informational
**Confidence:** High
**Category:** Emotional Journey
**Evidence:** 
"Yours. Your data stays on your own instance."

**Current Experience:**
When a privacy-conscious user discovers this app, their journey goes from:
1. **Shock/Amusement:** At the domain name.
2. **Intrigue:** At the "No ads, no gamification" promise.
3. **Relief:** Seeing the Docker deployment instructions and realizing they can actually own their data.
4. **Trust:** Seeing the clinical UI and AGPL license.

**Why It Matters:**
The product perfectly executes the emotional arc required to convert a skeptic into an advocate.

**Suggested Direction:**
Positive finding.

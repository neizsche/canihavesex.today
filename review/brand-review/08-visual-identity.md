# Brand Review: Visual Identity

## Title: Avoidance of the "Pink Tax" Aesthetic

**Severity:** Informational
**Confidence:** High
**Category:** Visual Identity
**Evidence:** 
The application utilizes a dark-mode first design, relying on Red, Amber, and Emerald green as semantic status indicators rather than decorative colors.

**Current Experience:**
Many commercial fertility apps infantilize their users with excessive floral patterns, bright pinks, and cartoonish illustrations. This application looks like a professional SaaS dashboard.

**Why It Matters:**
The clean, objective visual identity reinforces the brand's position as a serious, scientifically grounded tool rather than a lifestyle app. It also broadens the audience to include partners and male self-hosters who are deploying the app for their spouses.

**Suggested Direction:**
Positive finding. Do not introduce decorative themes.

**Estimated Impact:** High
**Estimated Effort:** N/A
**Launch Blocker:** No

# Brand Review: Naming

## Title: The Provocative Domain Name

**Severity:** Opportunity
**Confidence:** Confirmed
**Category:** Naming
**Evidence:** 
`canihavesex.today`

**Current Experience:**
The name is highly memorable. However, some users may feel uncomfortable having an app named "canihavesex.today" visible on their phone's home screen or browser history.

**Why It Matters:**
The name is brilliant for top-of-funnel marketing and initial virality (e.g., a Hacker News launch). However, it creates friction for daily use in public spaces.

**Suggested Direction:**
The inclusion of "Discreet Mode" in the app perfectly mitigates the internal UI issue. For the PWA installation, ensure the `manifest.json` specifies a highly discreet "Short Name" (e.g., just "Today" or "C.Today" with an abstract icon) so the app icon on the user's phone does not broadcast the full domain name.

**Estimated Impact:** High
**Estimated Effort:** Small
**Launch Blocker:** No

# Brand Review: First Impression

## Title: Provocative and Instantly Understood

**Severity:** Informational
**Confidence:** High
**Category:** First Impression
**Evidence:** 
The repository name is `canihavesex.today`. The primary tagline in the README is: "It answers one honest question — am I likely fertile today?"

**Current Experience:**
A visitor landing on the repository immediately knows exactly what the software does. There is zero corporate jargon ("empowering your wellness journey").

**Why It Matters:**
In a crowded market of health apps with vague, ethereal names (Flo, Clue, Glow), this product cuts through the noise with startling, memorable directness.

**Suggested Direction:**
Positive finding. Do not soften the tagline. Lean into the directness.

**Estimated Impact:** High
**Estimated Effort:** N/A
**Launch Blocker:** No

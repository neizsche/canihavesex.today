# Brand Review: Differentiation

## Title: Absolute Privacy via Self-Hosting

**Severity:** Informational
**Confidence:** Confirmed
**Category:** Differentiation
**Evidence:** 
"The easiest path is Docker. One app container + Postgres..."

**Current Experience:**
The primary differentiator is architectural. Rather than promising "we won't sell your data," the product promises "we literally cannot access your data because you host it yourself."

**Why It Matters:**
This shifts the trust model from "Trust our corporate policy" to "Trust the open-source code and your own server." For the `r/selfhosted` and Hacker News crowds, this is the ultimate differentiator.

**Suggested Direction:**
Ensure that if a hosted SaaS version is eventually launched, the self-hosted version remains a first-class citizen so the brand's core differentiation isn't compromised.

**Estimated Impact:** High
**Estimated Effort:** N/A
**Launch Blocker:** No

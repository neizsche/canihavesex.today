# Design Review: First Impression

## Title: Clear, Unambiguous Value Proposition

**Severity:** Informational
**Confidence:** High
**Category:** First Impression
**Affected Screens:** `TodayScreen.tsx`
**Evidence:** 
The primary screen (`TodayScreen`) prominently displays one of three states: "Highly Fertile", "Not Sure", or "Not Fertile". 

**User Impact:**
New users do not have to dig through complex calendar views to find the answer to their primary question. The app respects the user's time by providing an immediate, color-coded (Red/Amber/Emerald) answer.

**Suggested Direction:**
Positive finding. Maintain this "headline first" approach.

**Estimated Effort:** N/A
**Release Blocker:** No

# Design Review: Health Application UX

## Title: Avoidance of Gamification

**Severity:** Informational
**Confidence:** Confirmed
**Category:** Health Application UX
**Affected Screens:** Global
**Evidence:** 
There are no "streaks", "badges", or "points" awarded for logging data consecutively.

**User Impact:**
Health applications often mistakenly copy social media gamification. For fertility tracking, missing a day is normal biology, not a failure of character. By avoiding gamification, the application reduces the anxiety associated with "breaking a streak" and maintains a calm, clinical, and respectful tone.

**Suggested Direction:**
Positive finding. Do not implement daily streaks.

**Release Blocker:** No

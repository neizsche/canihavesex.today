# Design Review: Design System Consistency

## Title: Predictable Interactions

**Severity:** Informational
**Confidence:** Confirmed
**Category:** Design System
**Affected Screens:** Global
**Evidence:** 
The UI relies exclusively on standard, predictable native-like interaction patterns (bottom sheets for coaching, date navigators for time travel, primary full-width buttons for saving).

**User Impact:**
Users do not have to learn custom gestures or decipher novel icon meanings. The interaction patterns map directly to mental models established by iOS and Android system apps. 

**Suggested Direction:**
Positive finding. Avoid introducing custom swipe gestures or non-standard FABs (Floating Action Buttons) unless absolutely necessary.

**Estimated Effort:** N/A
**Release Blocker:** No
